    Prerequisites

    Some of the available features rely on our Dynamics Additions eCommerce solution being installed:

    • Repeat an entire order

    • Quickly re-order any previously ordered item

    All other features are available out of the box with very little setup required and do not require the full eCommerce solution.

    To Pay Invoices/Account Balances, you will need to have a payment provider account set up, for example with PayPal, SagePay, WorldPay or Realex.
